## 🔹 Blue print Details

1. **Chief Mentor → Co-Mentor App**

   * Sets framework, approves roadmaps, monitors progress.

2. **Co-Mentor App**

   * Dashboard highlights at-risk students.
   * Suggests interventions.
   * Collaborates with co-mentors.

3. **Student 360 View**

   * Full view of skills, milestones, projects, evidence.

4. **Daily → Weekly → Monthly → Quarterly Plans**

   * Micro-actions → Adaptive weekly steering → Milestone delivery → Career roadmap.

5. **Evidence Engine**

   * Captures proof from projects, assessments, mentor ratings, and employer feedback.

6. **Placement Probability & Intervention Engine**

   * Computes probability scores.
   * Recommends mentor actions to increase employability.

7. **Employer Matching & Placement Outcome**

   * Connects students with employers.
   * Signals verified talent ready for placement.


## 🔹 Key Features Highlighted

* **Mentor-centric**: Interventions, ROI tracking, collaboration.
* **Student-centric**: Adaptive learning, measurable progress, evidence collection.
* **System intelligence**: Probability model, risk flags, skill analytics.
* **Employer alignment**: Shortlisting, fit scoring, placement pipeline.
