# 🏢 Employer (Recruiter) Dashboard Structure

This structure represents the **Employer / Recruiter workflow** in the platform.  
It shows how recruiters can **manage company information, post jobs, evaluate candidates, conduct assessments and interviews, and track hiring results**.

---

# 📌 Main Sections

The Employer dashboard consists of **six major modules**:

1. 🏢 Company Profile  
2. 💼 Job Management  
3. 👥 Candidate Pool  
4. 🧠 Assessment  
5. 🎤 Interview  
6. 📊 Hiring Reports  

---

# 🏢 Company Profile

Stores the **basic identity of the company**.

### Fields
- 🏷️ Company Profile (Name)
- 🖼️ Company Logo
- ✨ Company Tagline
- 🏭 Industry Domain
- 📅 Founded Year
- 📍 Headquarters Location
- 🌐 Website URL

**Purpose:**  
Allows employers to represent their **organization and brand** on the platform.

---

# 💼 Job Management

Handles the **creation and management of job postings**.

## ✏️ Post Job
Define job details and publish vacancies.

- 📄 Basic Job Details
- 📝 Job Description
- 📍 Location & Work Mode
- 💰 Compensation
- ⚙️ Application Settings
- 👨‍💼 Number of Vacancies

## 📋 Manage Job
Track and control job postings.

- 📃 Job List
- 📊 Job Status
- ⚡ Job Actions (Edit / Close / View)

## 📥 Job Applications
Manage applicants.

- 👥 All Applicants
- ⭐ Shortlisted Candidates
- ❌ Rejected Candidates

---

# 👥 Candidate Pool

Acts as a **talent discovery database**.

Recruiters can explore candidates **even before they apply for jobs**.

### Features
- 👤 All Candidates
- 🔍 Search Candidate
- 🧩 Skill Filtering
- 🔄 Interview Pipeline

---

# 🧠 Assessment

Used for **skill evaluation of candidates**.

### Components
- 📚 Assessment Library
- 📤 Assign Assessments
- 📊 Assessment Results
- ⭐ Shortlisted Candidates
- ⏳ Ongoing Assessment *(optional)*
- 📑 Skill Evaluation Report *(optional)*

---

# 🎤 Interview

Manages the **candidate interview process**.

### Sections
- 📋 Interview Summary
- 📅 Upcoming Interviews
- 🕒 Today's Interviews
- 📊 Interview Status
- ✅ Interview Result
- ⭐ Candidate Shortlist

---

# 📊 Hiring Reports

Provides **final hiring analytics and results**.

### Reports
- 📈 Hiring Summary
- 🧾 Joining Status

---

# 🔄 Overall Hiring Flow

```text
Company Profile
      ↓
Post Job
      ↓
Candidates Apply / Candidate Pool Search
      ↓
Assign Assessments
      ↓
Evaluate Assessment Results
      ↓
Schedule Interviews
      ↓
Select Candidates
      ↓
Hiring Reports
